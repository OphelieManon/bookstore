Book Detail

When nineteen-year-old huntress Feyre kills a wolf in the woods, a terrifying creature arrives to demand retribution. Dragged to a treacherous magical land she knows about only from legends, Feyre discovers that her captor is not truly a beast, but one of the lethal, immortal faeries who once ruled her world.
As she adapts to her new home, her feelings for the faerie, Tamlin, transform from icy hostility into a fiery passion that burns through every lie she's been told about the beautiful, dangerous world of the Fae. But something is not right in the faerie lands. An ancient, wicked shadow is growing, and Feyre must find a way to stop it, or doom Tamlin-and his world-forever.

Review

By Elle , April 10, 2023

Okay, so. I thought this was going to be a MLM smut book for some reason? It wasn’t, LMAO. What it was was the most stunningly written novel I’ve ever had the spectacular pleasure of reading. It’s achingly dark and vivid, and yet, transcendently, euphorically bright and beautiful. What a ride. I feel like this author must sincerely relish being alive - who else could describe nature with such poetry? Who else can alchemize nouns and adjectives into balms and ambrosia?
