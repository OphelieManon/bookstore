Book Detail

A cute and entertaining tale of a brave pup named Winston and his adventures abroad. With the use of full-color photographs, spark your child's curiosity of the world as they tag along with Winston, visiting popular sights in London, Paris, and Italy. Children will learn the importance of embracing different cultures, cityscapes and cuisines, all the while making new friends along the way. Pack your imaginary bags and let's get ready for an adventure!

Review

By Kristina, March 23, 2023

Winston is just the most precious and photogenic Pom. This would be a fun book to help make traveling more relatable for littles. This would also be a great gift for any Pom lover. Can't wait to see where Winston's travels take him and who he might meet along the way.
